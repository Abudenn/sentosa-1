SENTOSA!

Sentosa is a popular tourist destination which now homes to a popular resort that receives more than twenty million visitors per year with many attraction to go to with your family and friends!

 

SILOSO BEACH

Siloso is a popular town beach in Singapore with well-developed infrastructure and great service. There are many cafes, restaurants, discos, night clubs and attractions here. Volleyball courts are set up on the coast, and the resort is popular among young tourists and those who like loud fun vacations!

   

SKYLINE LUGE

Skyline Luge and Skyride is a unique Singapore attraction! A fun outdoor experience on both the Jungle and Dragon Trail during day or night! You have full control over how fast and slow you would like your ride to be. On the Skyride, enjoy the scenic view of Sentosa Island and the Singapore coastline.

   

UNIVERSAL STUDIOS SINGAPORE

Immerse yourself in your favorite movies with exciting rides, high-thrill coasters, and interactive shows at Universal Studios Singapore. It features 28 rides, shows, and attractions in seven themed zones.
